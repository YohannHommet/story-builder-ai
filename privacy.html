Privacy Policy

Your child's privacy and safety matter to us

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Story Builder AI, we believe that children's creativity should flourish in a safe, protected environment. Our privacy policy reflects our commitment to protecting your family's privacy while providing an enriching storytelling experience.

Information We Collect

Story Creation Data:

  • Words provided for story generation (nouns, verbs, adjectives)
  • Generated stories
  • Basic usage statistics

We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal information about children
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device information

How We Use Information

  • To generate age-appropriate stories
  • To improve our story generation algorithm
  • To ensure content remains child-friendly
  • To fix technical issues

Data Protection

We implement multiple layers of protection:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Content filtering systems
  • Limited data retention

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with applicable laws. Our practices include:

  • No collection of personal information from children
  • Strict content filtering
  • Parent/guardian oversight requirements
  • No third-party sharing of information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any stored data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@storybuilder.ai

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes.
